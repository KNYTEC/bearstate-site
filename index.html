How we work

Four steps.
No surprises.

Honest scope, honest pricing, honest schedule. The job we quote is the job we pour — and the owner is on every site, every day.

01

Walk & Quote

On-site consult to measure, scope, and talk finishes. You get a written, line-item quote within 48 hours.

02

Plan & Permit

We coordinate with your designer, GC, or city for permits and engineering. No surprises in front of the framers.

03

Pour & Finish

Demo, form, pour, and finish — handled by our crew with the owner on-site. Tooled joints, clean lines, sealed and cured.

04

Walk & Warrant

Final walk-through with you. Two-year written warranty on workmanship, and a phone call answered when you need us.

Two decades in

A small crew.
A long memory.

Bear State Concrete Construction was started in 2006 on a simple idea: keep the crew small, the standards high, and the owner on every job site.

Twenty years later, we still run that way. We don't sub the finish work, we don't pad the scope, and we don't take on more pours than we can lead in person. That's why builders keep our number on speed dial — and why homeowners refer us to their neighbors.
